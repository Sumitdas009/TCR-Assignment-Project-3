About our cafe

Craving some delicious Greek food? Maybe you’re in the mood for a juicy steak? No matter what kind of meal you have in mind, The Engineer's Kitchen is ready to prepare it for you. Since 2019, The Engineer's Kitchen has been the go-to diner for residents of Bhubaneswar,Odisha. Our diner serves breakfast all day, in addition to wholesome and flavorful dining options for lunch and dinner. From starters to main course, non-veg to sea foods, you’ll find all kinds of hearty meals prepared fresh at The Engineer's Kitchen. Sounds delicious, right?

We’re here to serve you the best food around, whenever you’re looking for a great Indian restaurant in Bhubaneswar, Odisha.
